Congratulations! You successfuly configured game profile and started emulation. Emulation has two important modes - play and design. In play mode you can interact with your game. Design mode pauses game and you can customize your game layout - you can add various on-screen controls.
Press 'back' button on your device to show 'General options' with various settings.
