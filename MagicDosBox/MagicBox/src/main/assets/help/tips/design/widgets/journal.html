Journal

This allows you to take simple notes in your game. This widget has no special settings.
The Journal can be
•Saved - saves written text.
•Closed - closes the journal, releasing resources and forgets the cursor and scroll position.
•Hidden - journal is only hidden and remembers the cursor and scroll position. Note: Display rotation re-creates the journal and position will be forgotten!

Version 40 adds feature for searching keywords in the texts. Please note, journal widget was not made for very long texts.
