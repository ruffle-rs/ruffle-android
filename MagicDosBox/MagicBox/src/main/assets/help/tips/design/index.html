Design Mode

Welcome to design mode! This special mode allows you to add on-screen buttons (widgets) to your layout. Widgets are highly configurable buttons with dozens of functions. They can be placed, resized and styled to your needs.
How can I add a widget on the screen?
Long-press an empty space on the screen for a while. After 1 second the widget menu will appear.
How can I edit an existing widget?
Double tapping on a widget will display its properties.
How can I move a widget to another position?
Unpin widget with a long-press and drag it where you want. For precision movement use the floating toolbar menu arrows.
Don't forget to save your layout when you're done!
